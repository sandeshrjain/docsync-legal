# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-07

DocSync is a clinic communication and appointment reminder application operated by **Morphed AI**.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using DocSyn.

---

## Information We Collect

DocSync may collect and process the following information:
- Phone numbers provided by clinics for patient communication
- Message content related to appointment reminders and notifications
- Basic usage metadata required for message delivery

DocSync does not collect sensitive personal health records.

---

## How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:
- Send appointment reminders and notifications via WhatsApp
- Maintain delivery logs and system reliability
- Provide customer support to clinics

---

## Data Sharing

Data is not sold or shared with third parties.
Message delivery relies on WhatsApp (Meta) infrastructure as required to provide the service.

---

## Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide messaging services or as required by law.

---

## User Rights

Users may request access to or deletion of their data at any time.

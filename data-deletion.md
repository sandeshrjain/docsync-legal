# User Data Deletion Instructions

DocSyn is operated by **Morphed AI**.

Users may request deletion of their personal data at any time.

---

## How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of data associated with DocSyn:
1. Send an email to **info@morphedai.org**
2. Include your phone number and the word “Data Deletion” in the subject line

---

## What Happens Next

Upon verification:
- Associated phone numbers and message records will be deleted
- Deletion will be completed within a reasonable timeframe

---

If you have questions, contact:
**info@morphedai.org**
